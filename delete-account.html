Delete Account

Permanent deletion · No questions asked

This is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your cloud-synced data cannot be recovered. Export your data first if you want to keep a copy.
Your local notes and library will remain on your device until you delete the app. Only cloud-synced data is removed from our servers.
How to delete your account
1
Export your data (optional)
Go to Settings → Export data to save a full JSON backup of your library — books, notes, links, and graph layout. This is the only way to recover your data after deletion.
2
Send a deletion request
Email hello@usesynapse.io with the subject line "Account deletion request". Include the email address associated with your Synapse account. We will confirm receipt within 1–2 business days.
3
Confirmation
We'll send you a confirmation email when your account has been deleted from Firebase and your cloud data has been queued for permanent removal from our servers.

In-app account deletion (Settings → Delete account) is coming in a future update.

What gets deleted
Permanently deleted
  • Your Firebase Auth account and credentials
  • All synced books and source metadata
  • All synced notes and note content
  • All note links and graph connections
  • Saved graph layout positions
  • Your user record in our PostgreSQL database
Stays on your device
  • Local Hive database (on-device copy of your library)
  • App files and cache

To remove local data, delete the Synapse app from your device — this wipes the on-device Hive database along with all app files.

Deletion timeline
Immediately
Your Firebase Auth account is disabled. You will be signed out of all devices and unable to sign back in.
Within 30 days
All cloud-synced data (books, notes, note links, graph layout) is permanently deleted from our PostgreSQL database on Railway. No backups are retained after this point.
Per Google's policy
Firebase Auth logs and metadata are deleted according to Google's own retention schedule, typically within 180 days. See the Firebase Privacy Policy ↗.
Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, email hello@usesynapse.io. We'll respond within 1–2 business days.
